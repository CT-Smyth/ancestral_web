Preserving the cultural heritage of our world 

At Ancestral.al, we work to preserve the memetic and linguistic cultural diversity of indigenous cultures worldwide.
Preservation

Ancestral.al can help to preserve and share your tribe's cultural wisdom, beliefs, and practices, ensuring that your culture can be remembered and respected by present and future generations alike.

Data Sovereignty 

Our cultural avatars are locally hosted on private, state of the art models. Self hosting and cloud hosting solutions keep tribal organizations in control of their data and privacy. We do not collect, sell, or use private information from users.

A Bridge to the Future

Ancestral.al interactive cultural avatars not only educate people about your tribe's culture, history, and traditions but can also connect your tribe's unique cultural identity to young people, scholars, and educators worldwide. 

Believe in the wisdom of your cultural heritage. 

We believe that the wisdom of elders is more relevant now than ever, as people everywhere face challenges that will determine the course of humanity for centuries to come. Keep your voice alive and available for the youth and leaders of tomorrow.
